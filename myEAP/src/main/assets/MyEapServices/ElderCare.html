Eldercare

Cascade’s Eldercare services provides assistance in finding solutions to the aging needs of older adults and the family members caring for them such as housing, alternative living, home health, community services, legal concerns and medical issues. We help employees and their families through the often confusing maze of services associated with aging, sudden chronic illness, or disability. Packets of information are sent to the caller within 48 hours of intake assessment.

  • Health Care Options
  • Hospitals and Skilled Nursing
  • Transitional and Long-Term Care
  • Veterans and Rehabilitation Hospitals
  • Mail-Order Pharmacies
  • Living Concerns
  • Retirement Communities
  • Assisted and Independent Living
  • Alzheimer’s Facilities
