Personal Wellness

Through Cascade EAP, you have immediate access to professional health coaches who provide unlimited solution-focused health and wellness coaching, and referrals to local resources. You can receive on-going coaching, support, and information from a healthcare professional.

Areas include:

  • Weight Management
  • Smoking Cessation
  • Stress Management
  • Nutrition & Healthy Cooking
  • Pre & Post Natal Care
  • Self- Care of Chronic Conditions
